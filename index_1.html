About

Enterprise sales leader with a global footprint

I've spent 20 years selling complex technology into some of the world's most skeptical buyers — banks, financial institutions, and regulated enterprises across the US, MENA, LATAM, APAC, and Africa. The roles that have energized me most have always been the disruptive ones.

Biometrics before it was mainstream. Digital identity before compliance teams knew they needed it. KYC/AML platforms before regulators mandated them. I've always been drawn to technology that changes the rules of an industry rather than just improving on what already exists.

I'm now focused on AI — the most significant disruptive technology of my lifetime — and specifically on bringing it credibly into the markets I know best: financial institutions, governments, and regulated enterprises across the globe.

I know how to take something genuinely disruptive and make it feel inevitable rather than threatening. That's been the common thread across every role I've held.
